About Us

Welcome to Kokomochi Hibachi Express

We are a family-owned restaurant proudly serving the Kokomo, Indiana community. At Kokomochi Hibachi Express, we are committed to bringing you the highest quality sushi and hibachi, crafted with care and passion.

Our mission is to provide not just delicious food, but an exceptional dining experience that brings friends and families together. Whether you're enjoying a flavorful hibachi meal or savoring our fresh sushi rolls, we want every visit to be memorable.

What sets us apart:

  • Fresh, high-quality ingredients in every dish
  • A cozy and inviting family-friendly atmosphere
  • Dedicated staff ensuring excellent service
  • Signature hibachi meals that are grilled to perfection
  • Fresh sushi rolls with a variety of options to suit every taste

We look forward to welcoming you into our dining room and sharing our love for authentic flavors with you. Come and experience Kokomochi Hibachi Express—where great food and great company come together.
